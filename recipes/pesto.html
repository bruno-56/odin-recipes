Pesto sauce:

Delicious and easy

Make your meal tastier with pesto sauce!

Ingredients:

  • Basil leaves
  • Pine nuts
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Hard cheese(s)

Steps:

  1. Pulse nuts and garlic in a food processor
  2. Add fresh basil leaves, salt and pepper
  3. Blend into a paste
  4. Slowly pour in Olive oil to emulsify the sauce
  5. Add your preference cheese
  6. Store and serve
