Risotto al Parmigiano

Delicious and easy

Make yout meal tastier with Risotto al Parmigiano

Ingredients:

  • Arborio Rice
  • Hot stock
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps:

  1. Sweat the rice
  2. Add stock gradually
  3. Cook to Al Dente
  4. Mantecatura
  5. Rest and serve
